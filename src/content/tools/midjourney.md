## What it does

Midjourney is an AI image generator built by **Midjourney, Inc.** Launched as a Discord bot in July 2022, a web app was added in 2024. By 2026, it has **21 million+ registered users** and roughly **$500 million in annual revenue** — with a team of just over 100.

In May 2026, **Midjourney V8.1** (released April 30, 2026) is the latest release: HD 2K image output, faster generation, improved prompt adherence, and Raw mode options. **V7** remains the default version in docs, bringing **Omni Reference** for character consistency and photorealism gains over V6. Midjourney also supports **image-to-video** generation: a still image can become a 5–21 second video.

Midjourney's edge: **aesthetic quality**. Cinematic portraits, fantasy landscapes, concept art, sci-fi scenes — it leads the industry on mood-heavy, stylized imagery. It collapses hours of concept art into minutes.

## Pricing

- **Basic ($10/mo)** — 3.3 hours Fast GPU, ~200 images/month
- **Standard ($30/mo)** — 15 hours Fast, **unlimited Relax mode**, best value for individuals
- **Pro ($60/mo)** — 30 hours Fast, **Stealth mode** (private generations)
- **Mega ($120/mo)** — 60 hours Fast, Stealth, highest concurrency

Annual billing gives 20% off. **No free tier.**

## Capabilities

- High-fidelity image generation from text prompts
- Omni Reference for character consistency
- Photorealism (V7+) and stylized art (Niji)
- Parameter system: `--style`, `--stylize`, `--chaos`, `--weird`
- Image-to-image with prompt guidance
- Upscaling and variation generation
- Web app + Discord bot
- V1 video generation (limited rollout)

## Strengths

- Most aesthetically capable image generator in 2026
- Excels at cinematic, fantasy, and concept art
- V7/V8 photorealism rivals dedicated photo models
- Fast — minutes vs hours of traditional concept art
- Strong Discord community (21M+ users)

## Weaknesses

- No free tier — paid subscription required
- Less reliable for photorealistic people, technical illustration, product renders
- Steep learning curve — parameters and prompt engineering needed
- Copyright and ethical concerns around AI-generated art
- Discord-first UX still feels clunky despite improved web app

## Ecosystem

Midjourney's ecosystem lives on Discord: a 21M-user official server for prompt and output sharing, style discovery, community tutorials. There's no third-party marketplace — value concentrates in the model quality and Discord community.

## What it does

ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is the world's most widely used AI assistant. Since its November 2022 launch, it has become the **reference point** for the AI industry. Available via web, mobile apps, desktop apps, and API.

In 2026, OpenAI positioned ChatGPT around the **GPT-5.x family**. As of May 2026, **GPT-5.5** (released April 24, 2026) is the flagship for highest-quality work, **GPT-5.4** remains the cost-balanced alternative, and **GPT-5.2-Codex** powers dedicated coding workflows. The GPT-4o generation has given way to faster, smarter models.

ChatGPT's strengths: a huge ecosystem (thousands of custom GPTs), native multimodal capabilities (text + voice + image + file), long-term **memory**, and the smoothest mobile experience in the industry.
